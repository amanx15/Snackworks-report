A note from the team

This guide has two parts: universal rules for every Snackworks video, and product-specific direction for each of the four current SKUs. Read your assigned product's section before you film, run the checklist before you submit, and you're set.

Part one

Universal rules

These apply to every Snackworks video. Tap any rule to expand.

01
Must do
#ad overlay — full duration, prominent
+

#ad must appear on screen for the entire duration — not just at the start.

"#ad" as a text overlay, full duration, easy to spot.
"#add" (typo), "Ad", or hidden under your watermark.
02
Must do
Verbal partnership disclosure in the first 10 seconds
+

You must say out loud that you've partnered with the brand within the first 10 seconds. Captions alone don't count.

"I partnered with OREO…" / "SOUR PATCH KIDS sent me…"
Skip the verbal callout. Note: brand is OREO singular — never "OREOs".
For ASMR or no-voiceover videos, disclosure must appear in the caption AND as a clear text overlay.
03
Must do
Required hashtags and handles at the start of caption
+

Hashtags and handles must appear at the beginning of your caption. @snackworksofficial and #ad are required for all four products. See the product cards below for exact tags per SKU.

04
Must do
Commercial Music Library only
+

Sound MUST be sourced from TikTok's Commercial Music Library. Trending songs, third-party audio, artwork, or any copyrighted work = video rejected.

05
Must do
Never consume product directly from the packaging
+
Reach into bag → CUT → eating shot. The edit shows portion control.
Pull product straight from the bag to your mouth in one continuous shot.
06
Must do
One serving max — check the nutrition label
+

Showing or consuming more than one serving = automatic flag.

SPK Chews = 7–9 pieces per serving. CHIPS AHOY! = 2 cookies. When in doubt, check the label.
07
Must do
No overindulgence or "craving" / "addicting" language
+
"So good" / "Really enjoyed these" / "A great snack"
"I could eat the whole pack" / "Craving" / "Addicting" / Mukbang content
08
Must do
No superlatives — "perfect", "ideal", "ultimate"
+
"Perfect for movie night" / "Perfect for a watch party" (occasion-based is fine)
"Perfect amount of creme" / "Ideal flavor" / "Ultimate cookie"
09
Must do
No unapproved health claims
+
Talk about taste, fun, occasions, sharing, flavor.
"Healthy" / "Vegan" / "Clean" / "Guilt-free" / Any health condition references
10
Must do
No false pricing or urgency claims
+
Mention a discount only if it's confirmed active.
"Last day of the deal" / "Won't find a cheaper price in store" / "Only until midnight"
11
Heads up
No third-party logos, products, or copyrights
+
Plain backgrounds. Generic clothing. Cover tattoos if no IP rights.
Branded apparel (Nike, sports teams), competitor products, alcohol, drugs, violence.
12
Heads up
No art in the background
+

Paintings, prints, posters, murals — all copyright concerns. Plain walls or blurred backgrounds only.

13
Heads up
No identifiable people without a written release
+

Anyone identifiable on camera needs a written release. Children under 13 cannot consume or interact with the product.

14
Heads up
No movies, events, celebrities, or cultural moments
+

Don't reference movies, event titles, celebrity names, or social moments. This includes the World Cup.

15
Heads up
No unsafe handling or unhygienic food practices
+
Open with hands or rounded scissors. Use food-safe surfaces.
Open fire (incl. candles). Microwave packages. Catch-in-mouth tricks.
16
Heads up
No pets, kids<13, or animals near product
+

Don't show pets or children under 13 consuming or interacting with the product.

17
Heads up
No religion, politics, or sensitive topics
+

No references to religion, politics, racism, gender inequality, or sexual content. No foul language.

18
Good to know
No third-party recipes or ingredient amounts
+

Don't use someone else's recipe as your own. Don't include ingredient amounts or serving suggestions in recipe content.

The products

Each SKU has its own bundle, hooks, hashtags, and brand-specific rules.

Product 01 · Sour Patch Kids

SOUR PATCH KIDS Original

Bundle
  • SOUR PATCH KIDS Original Soft & Chewy CandyThe classic — sour, then sweet.
Caption
Copy and paste at the start of your caption
#ad @therealsourpatchkids @snackworksofficial
Say on camera (first 10 sec)
"I partnered with the SOUR PATCH KIDS brand…"
Content ideas
Macro shot opener
Open with a close-up of the candy. The colors and shapes earn a rewatch.
"Crowning my favorite flavor"
Line them up, crown a winner. Bring in a guest (with consent) — parents or first-timers work great.
"Make a candy salad with me"
Pour into a container and mix. Emphasize sweet-sour balance and sharing occasions. Avoid eating candy on camera.
Brand rules
  • Chews have SOUR on the inside — NOT "sugar in the middle".
  • Red flavor is Redberry — NOT "strawberry".
No LIVES — please!
